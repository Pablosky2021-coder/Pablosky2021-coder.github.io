Essential Peripherals for Video Editing

A great PC needs the best tools to complete your professional setup. We recommend these high-quality peripherals for the best video editing experience.

Professional Color Monitor

A minimum 27-inch 4K monitor with 10-bit color depth (or 100% DCI-P3 coverage) is essential for accurate color grading and final look consistency.

High-Precision Mouse & Tablet

A weighted, high DPI mouse offers precise control for frame-accurate editing. A graphics tablet is perfect for detailed color correction and mask work.

External Backup Storage (RAID)

Fast external RAID storage (Thunderbolt/USB 4) is critical for project archiving and local backup, ensuring data safety and rapid file access.
